Let's Chat About Programming

This page is more than just an exploration of my sewing projects -- believe me, there's more than this!

Rather, it's also an exploration of web development techniques and more specifically, a project revolving around Bootstrap.

Everything from the jumbotron and the navigation bar to the individual cards displaying sewing projects has been styled using Bootstrap.

By no means is it meant to look perfect, but rather be a quick exploration of potential styles and options.

And it's all responsive :)
